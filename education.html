Understanding Vein Colors

Kratom (Mitragyna Speciosa) leaves come in different vein colors based on maturity and processing. Each variety offers unique characteristics for botanical research:

Red Vein

The most mature leaves. Known for their deep, rich coloration. Popular for evening research and relaxation studies.

Green Vein

Mid-maturity leaves. Balanced alkaloid profile. Favored by researchers studying focus and productivity.

White Vein

Youngest leaves. Light coloration. Often used in morning routine studies and energy research.

Gold/Yellow

Specialty fermented or dried leaves. Unique processing creates distinct characteristics. Rare and sought after by collectors.
